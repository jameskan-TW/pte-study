#1 生活經驗vs學校正規教育 #070001 Argument
英文題目

Some people think that life experience is more important than formal education provided in schools and

universities. How far do you agree with this statement, and provide examples?

中文翻譯

有些人認爲，生活經驗比學校和大學提供的正規教育更重要。你在多大程度上同意這種說法？請舉例說明。

範文

It is generally true that whether having experience or education is more important has become a staple

of discussion over the years, and this has caused great controversy and drawn the attention from the

general public. Some people are of the opinion that having experience can be more advantageous,

while others hold the view that being educated in schools can be more crucial. I tend to agree more

with the former and will try to elaborate below.

It has to be admitted that formal education plays a more significant role in our life, and one argument

in favor of this view can be that this can help people to gain theoretical and practical knowledge. If we

can receive formal education, then we can achieve outstanding academic performance, as well as

developing a wide range of skills, such as critical thinking and analytical skills, in turn leading to us

having better future prospects. Given this, it is unsurprising that formal education can bring tangible

and intangible benefits to students’ future success.

On the other hand, I feel affirmative that experience can be more critical in the future, and one

compelling reason may well be that having experience can result in significant time-savings. A

pertinent case in point is James, who works for a world renowned and prestigious firm called

Microsoft. As James is fully experienced in terms of artificial intelligence and programming, this can

help him to improve efficiency and boost productivity. What this means is that he can stand better

chances of being promoted to higher managerial hierarchies within the company, or potentially make

more money shifting to Facebook or Amazon.

In conclusion, despite the fact that the counter argument may hold true in many cases, I feel that

experience is more pivotal regarding personal success.
