PTE 作文模板 · 考前封關卡

James 專用 · 藍底＝slot（考場現填）· 黃底＝你最常掉的字

一、完整模板

P1 · 開場（兩派觀點 + 表態）

It is generally true that 主題 has become a matter of concern over the years, and this has caused great controversy and captured public attention. Some people are of the opinion that 觀點A, while others hold the view that 觀點B. I tend to agree more with the latter and will discuss this further below.

P2 · 讓步（承認反方）

It has to be admitted that 反方論點, and one argument in favor of this view can be that 理由. If 條件, then 主詞 can 結果A, in turn leading to 結果B. As a result, it is unsurprising that 小結.

P3 · 主論（我的立場 + James 例子）

On the other hand, I firmly believe that 我的立場, and one compelling reason is that 理由. A pertinent case in point is James, who works at a world-renowned and prestigious firm. As James 做了什麼, he is able to 結果. In other words, 含義.

P4 · 結論

In conclusion, despite the fact that the counterargument may hold true in many cases, I believe that 最終立場. It is crucial that 建議.

二、你的罩門清單（交卷前掃這幾項）

  • view（頭號）：兩個 view 都要在 — P1 hold the view that、P2 in favor of this view。曾連錯 5 次，打 P2 那句手指停一下確認。
  • and one ＿＿：P2 and one argument、P3 and one compelling reason — 開頭的 and 最常被吞，當一個音節「安－萬」一起打。
  • 吞小字：generally true that、has to be admitted、is able to、works at a — 這些 helper 字手快就掉。
  • hold true ≠ hold truth：true 是形容詞（成立），不是名詞 truth。
  • in other words：大寫 I + 複數 s；別跟 On the other hand（P3開頭）和 it is unsurprising that（P2結尾）搞混。
  • and this has caused：不要寫成 it has caused（this 指代主題比較清楚）。
  • P2 五拍別漏行：If → then → can → in turn leading to → As a result，少一拍就知道漏了。
  • 第三人稱 +s（填 slot 內容時）：he spends、James shops、a student surfs — 主詞單數動詞一定加 s。
  • 拼字三模式：①漏 r（large/further）②-ing 沒去 e（using/evolving）③字母顛倒（people）；還有 despite 別拼成 dispect。

三、記憶鉤

  • 每段第二句：P1 → a matter of concern；P2 → and one argument…can be；P3 → and one compelling reason…is；P4 → that the counterargument…
  • P2 軟 / P3 硬：反方用 argument…can be（留餘地），我方用 compelling reason…is（講死）。
  • opinion 先、view 後：Some people are of the opinion；while others hold the view；同意的是 latter（＝others 那邊）。
  • hand 配 On、words 配 In：開頭 On the other hand，結尾 In other words。

字數目標 200–300（約 250）· 寫完留 30 秒專掃：①動詞 +s ②view/小字 ③despite 拼字
