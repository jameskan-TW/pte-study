📚 五大論據英文句（背熟這些，37 題通吃）▼
①
效率 & 時間節省
可套的題：幾乎所有題（科技、商場、媒體、工時）
論點：[X] can help people save time and work better（進階：improve efficiency and boost productivity）
A. 省時：people can finish their work faster
B. 陪家人：they can spend more quality time with their families
C. 關鍵影響：this makes the family closer and happier
②
學業 & 未來前景
可套的題：教育、考試、兼職、語言、媒體
論點：[X] can help students achieve outstanding academic performance
A. 性格：they can build good habits, such as self-discipline
B. 自律：with self-discipline, they keep learning and growing
C. 關鍵影響：this leads them to a better future
③
身心健康 & 壓力
可套的題：工作壓力、媒體、考試、兒童（最常拿來 P2 讓步）
論點（反面）：[X] can lead to stress and anxiety
A. 煩躁：people easily feel upset and angry
B. 脾氣：they lose their temper over small things
C. 關鍵影響：they speak rudely to their families, which hurts family relationships
正面版（讓步用）：people feel relaxed and enjoy peace of mind
④
視野 & 文化理解
可套的題：語言、旅遊、媒體、戲劇、海外學習
論點：[X] can help people gain a broader view of life
A. 懂很多：they learn things they cannot find in books
B. 談吐：they become more interesting and confident when they talk
C. 關鍵影響：this helps them get along with people from different backgrounds
⑤
職涯 & 財務
可套的題：工作、婚姻、薪資、工時、名人
論點：[X] can bring people a good job and a steady income（進階：financial stability）
A. 不擔心錢：they do not need to worry about money
B. 心情穩：they can enjoy peace of mind
C. 關鍵影響：this creates a warm and stable home for the whole family
💡
記憶法：論據宇宙只有兩個終點
①③⑤ → 家人（省時→家人更親密／壓力→傷害家人／收入穩→家庭氣氛穩）
②④ → 個人成長（自律→更好的未來／視野→談吐與人緣）
考場上忘詞時，往「家人」或「個人成長」兩個方向想就對了。
