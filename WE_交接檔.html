1. 個人專屬模板（175字）▼
藍色 = 固定模板句，不用改。黃色 = slot，考試時填入自己的內容。

P1 引言

It is generally true that [topic] has become a matter of concern over the years, and this has caused great controversy and captured public attention. Some people are of the opinion that [view A], while others hold the view that [view B]. I tend to agree more with the latter and will discuss this further below.

P2 讓步段

It has to be admitted that [concession point], and one argument in favor of this view can be that [reason]. If [condition], then [subject] can [result], in turn leading to [consequence]. As a result, it is unsurprising that [outcome].

P3 主論段

On the other hand, I firmly believe that [main argument], and one compelling reason is that [reason]. A pertinent case in point is James, who works at a world-renowned and prestigious firm. As James [action/situation], he is able to [outcome]. In other words, [restate main point].

P4 結論

In conclusion, despite the fact that the counterargument may hold true in many cases, I believe that [final stance]. It is crucial that [recommendation].
2. 五大論點速查卡▼
所有 37 題的思路都能從這 5 類套出來。P2 用其中一類讓步，P3 用另一類主論。
論點核心概念常用句型
💪 效率／生產力 提升工作效率、節省時間、增加產出 can boost productivity / save time / improve performance
📚 學術／研究 研究證明、學術數據支持、認知表現 studies show / research suggests / evidence indicates
🏥 健康 身心健康、壓力、生活品質 improve physical and mental health / reduce stress / enhance wellbeing
🎭 文化／社會 社區認同、文化傳承、社會凝聚力 strengthen cultural identity / build community / preserve heritage
💼 職業／事業 職涯發展、就業機會、技能提升 advance career prospects / create job opportunities / develop skills
選論點原則：P2（讓步）選跟你主論相反的角度，P3（主論）選你最有把握的那一類。
3. 正反論述寫法（以「學術」論點為例）▼
題目如果問 positively or negatively，P2 寫反面讓步，P3 寫你支持的正面主論。

支持正面 → P2 寫負面讓步

It has to be admitted that buildings with bad air flow, little natural light, and too much noise can raise stress and harm people's health. If workers spend time in these kinds of places, then they can feel tired and find it hard to focus, in turn leading to a strong wish to move somewhere else.

支持正面 → P3 寫正面主論

Studies show that buildings with good lighting, fresh air, and quiet spaces help people think better and feel happier at work. If people live or work in these kinds of places, then they can stay focused and feel good each day, in turn leading to a stronger desire to stay in that location.

P3 James 例子（配正面）

A pertinent case in point is James, who works at a world-renowned and prestigious firm. As James works in a bright and well-designed office with plenty of natural light and open space, he is able to stay focused and feel good at work every day. In other words, the design of his workplace has made him want to stay there long term.
4. 套題示範：建築設計題▶ 展開
題目：How does the design of buildings affect, either positively or negatively, where people work or live?

立場分析

  • 支持：正面影響（好設計讓人想留下）
  • P2 讓步：負面（差設計讓人想離開）
  • P3 主論：正面（好設計提升健康/學術表現）
  • 論點選擇：學術論點（研究數據支撐）

思路結構

段落內容
P1建築設計對工作/居住地點選擇有正負面影響 → 我支持正面影響更顯著
P2讓步：差的設計（噪音、缺光）→ 壓力上升 → 人想離開
P3主論：好的設計（採光、通風）→ 認知表現提升 → 人選擇留下 → James 例子
P4結論：建築設計的正面影響對人的居住/工作選擇至關重要
5. 模板使用注意事項▶ 展開

固定不變的句子

  • 所有藍色模板句在 37 題裡都通用，不需要改
  • James 的名字固定，職場描述固定：works at a world-renowned and prestigious firm
  • 結論段幾乎不需要改，只換最後兩個 slot

容易出錯的地方

  • P1 最後一句：latter（後者）= 你支持的立場，確認邏輯方向
  • P2 開頭：It has to be admitted that = 讓步，內容要跟你的主論相反
  • P3：On the other hand 接主論，James 例子要跟 P3 論點一致
  • 字數目標：175字左右，不要超過 200 字

簡單字彙替換原則

不確定一個字對不對，換成更簡單的字。PTE 評分看邏輯結構，不看難字。
難字簡單替換
inadequate lightinglittle natural light
cognitive performanceability to think and focus
chronic fatiguefeeling tired all the time
ergonomically designedwell-designed / comfortable
