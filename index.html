🗣️ Speaking

RA · Read Aloud

  • 不給自己準備時間，拿到題目就開始讀
  • 流暢度比速度重要：語速平均、連貫清晰、適當停頓換氣
  • 詞與詞之間自然連讀，不要一個字一個字蹦出來
  • 逗號、句號、從句前、and 前都要短暫停頓
  • 練到非生字句子能完全流暢讀出

RS · Repeat Sentence

  • 不看答案直接練，每天至少 50 題，熟練後可達 100 題
  • 不做筆記以維持流暢度，記不住就多刷題
  • 注重流暢度而非完美內容
  • WFD 和 RS 都用常速練習，不加速

RL · Re-tell Lecture

  • 考前利用自我介紹那頁的 2 分鐘，把模板寫好
  • 聽懂哪句就記哪句，模板固定四句
  • 錄音結束後只有約 10 秒整理筆記，要快速組織

DI · Describe Image

  • 開頭念標題，結尾說結論
  • 折線圖：說上升下降趨勢
  • 柱狀圖：說大小與極值
  • 圓餅圖：從大到小描述
  • 流程圖：逐步說明步驟
  • 地圖或圖片：看到什麼說什麼

Short Dynamic Template（20–25 sec）

The [type] shows how [topic] changed from [year] to [year].
Overall, the trend shows a [steady increase / sharp drop / fluctuation].
[Category A] increased the most, while [Category B] showed little change.
In general, the graph highlights a clear shift over time.

Standard Dynamic Template（30–35 sec）

The graph illustrates the changes in [topic] over a period from [year] to [year].
It can be seen that [main trend] occurred during this time.
At the beginning, [starting point], but later it [rose / fell / stabilized].
Among the categories, [Category A] experienced the most significant change.
Overall, the chart indicates that [main conclusion].

趨勢描述詞彙

方向動詞 + 副詞組合
📈 上升rose steadily / increased gradually / grew significantly / climbed sharply / surged dramatically
📉 下降fell slightly / dropped suddenly / decreased steadily / declined gradually / plunged rapidly
➖ 持平remained constant / stayed unchanged / leveled off after [year]
🔁 波動fluctuated slightly / varied considerably / showed minor fluctuations
🎯 極值peaked at / hit a record high / bottomed out near zero

ASQ · Answer Short Question

  • 過一遍高頻題，約 100 題，不超過一小時
  • 分值低但容易得分，不能放棄

✍️ Writing

WE · Write Essay

  • 準備一份約 200 字的模板，練到 6 分鐘能完整打出
  • 考試時直接套用模板並留時間檢查
  • 文章不超過 300 字
  • 熟練打字速度（推薦：typingclub.com、keybr.com）

標準四段結構

段落開頭句
IntroductionNowadays, whether [topic] is beneficial or harmful to society has become a widely discussed issue... From my point of view, I firmly believe that [stance].
Body 1One of the main reasons why I support this view is that [reason]. For example, ... This shows that ...
Body 2On the other hand, some people may argue that ... However, I am convinced that this view overlooks... For instance, ... This demonstrates that ...
ConclusionIn conclusion, although there are different opinions, it is clear to me that ... If managed properly, this [approach] can continue to bring positive impacts in the future.

Quick Reminder

  • 開場：Nowadays, whether... is beneficial or harmful to society.
  • 立場：brings more advantages than disadvantages
  • 萬用優點：improves one's daily efficiency / saves a significant amount of time
  • 語法：without + V-ing
  • 結尾：is essential for maintaining a healthy work-life balance

逆向檢查法

從文章最後一字往回讀，打破大腦自動補全慣性，讓每個單字拼寫被獨立審視。

SWT · Summarize Written Text

  • 一邊讀一邊寫，抓各段的第一句或最後一句，用 and 連接
  • 不用 but、從句等複雜結構
  • 控制在 50 字左右並留時間檢查
  • 只能一句話（5–75 字）

How to find the key things?

  1. Find topic & main idea：通常在第一句，問「這段主要講什麼？」
  2. Find author's purpose：找文章要 explain / prove / describe 什麼
  3. Pick 2–3 supporting ideas：選廣泛且重複出現的概念
  4. 用連接詞組成 one smooth sentence

SWT Content 得分策略

  1. 快速找出主要觀點
  2. 選出 2–3 個支撐點
  3. 改寫（Paraphrase），不要原文複製
  4. 保持簡潔，省略次要例子
  5. 確保連貫，用連接詞讓摘要完整

📖 Reading

FIB · Fill in the Blanks (Drop Down)

  • 集中練高頻題，看到空前後幾個詞就能直覺選出
  • 熟悉常見搭配、語法結構

5 Tips for Success

  1. Read for Gist：先讀完整段，理解整體「故事」再填空
  2. Part of Speech：填空前判斷：這裡需要名詞、動詞還是形容詞？
  3. Collocations：英文有固定搭配，例如 remain open / research funding
  4. Grammar Clues：看空格前後——"a [blank]" 告訴你下一個詞是單數可數名詞
  5. Tone & Context：文章談「危機」→ 空格詞通常與問題、減少、負面結果有關

V-ing vs V-ed 判斷

情況用法例子
名詞發出動作V-ingresearch suggesting that...
名詞接受動作V-edresearch conducted by...

include / includes / including

用法主詞範例
include複數Some of the factors include...
includes單數The main factor includes...
including分詞（不能當主要動詞）...factors, including X, are important.

RO · Re-order Paragraphs

  • 只練高頻題，用死記硬背法記順序
  • 每篇做錯的提煉關鍵詞，中英雙語，編故事記順序

MCQ · Multiple Choice

  • 不特別練；考試時若時間充裕可仔細推敲
  • 多選不確定時只選一個，避免扣分

🎧 Listening

SST · Summarize Spoken Text

  • 聽高頻題音檔，自己練寫摘要再對答案
  • 用顏色標籤記錄沒聽出主題的題目，重點複習
  • WFD 做好了，SST 記筆記也會飛快

簡易摘要模板

The lecture explains [idea 1].
It also says [idea 2].
Finally, it highlights [idea 3].

WFD · Write from Dictation ⭐ 重中之重

  • 每天練 50 題以上，全部真題練多輪
  • 顏色標籤分類：🟢 全對 → 🟡 小錯 → 🔵 錯 → 🔴 特別難
  • 練習打字速度（推薦：typingclub.com、keybr.com），目標 50+ wpm
  • 要實際打字，不偷懶只口說；多猜詞不扣分但注意拼寫與大小寫
  • 保持常速練習，不加速

📐 Grammar Notes

長難句拆解法（4步驟）

  1. 找主幹：只找「主詞 + 動詞」，忽略所有修飾
  2. 標出修飾成分：which / that / who 子句、插入語
  3. 刪掉修飾語：句子瞬間清楚
  4. 再把修飾語加回去：理解每個修飾語在補充什麼
Although modern technology, which has rapidly transformed the way people communicate, provides convenience, it also creates new challenges that individuals must learn to manage.

→ 主幹：modern technology provides convenience and creates challenges

句子成分（主謂賓補）

成分功能問法範例
主詞 Subject誰在做事誰？什麼？Students study hard.
謂語 Verb做了什麼在幹嘛？study / changes / provides
受詞 Object動作作用在誰對誰？對什麼？Students read books.
補語 Complement補充說明—Technology is useful.
定語 Modifier(N)修飾名詞什麼樣的？哪個？significant improvement
狀語 Modifier(V)修飾動作怎麼？何時？study hard
💡 第一輪閱讀只抓主 + 謂，定語、狀語、補語全部先忽略

詞性出現的地方

詞性出現位置範例
名詞冠詞後：a/an/thethe concept
形容詞後significant improvement
介係詞後the importance of education
所有格後their contribution
動詞的主詞/受詞Pollution affects health.
形容詞連綴動詞後（become/get/grow/turn）become useful
副詞形容詞前extremely difficult task
一般動詞前後He always studies / speaks fluently
句首（修飾全句）Unfortunately, the weather changed.
完整句子中間（刪掉仍完整）No one is sure exactly what...

When to use "the"

用 "the"不用 "the"
世界唯一（the sun / the moon）泛指一般概念（Happiness is important.）
前文已提過同一個東西複數/不可數名詞泛指（Apples are healthy.）
用片語限定「哪一個」語言/運動/三餐（speak English / play tennis）
共同背景知識（the weather）school/work/home 泛指地點
最高級（the best）
💡 判斷口訣：對方知道我在說「哪一個」嗎？是 → 用 the；否 → 不加
